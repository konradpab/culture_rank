§ 1 — CIS Algorithm: Cultural Impact Score
CIS = (G × 1.5) + (C1.8) + (V × 1.2) + (I × 2.0) + (E × 1.5)
🔗 v2.1 upgrade: I can be auto-derived from the edge graph via PageRank. When edges exist, I_auto is computed and displayed alongside the manual score. Final I = max(I_manual, I_auto).

§ 3 — External Identifier Schema
Works carry authority-control identifiers enabling cross-archive linking. Canonical ID rule: ids.wikidata if present, else the internal id, else a hash of {title, creator, era}. Supported registries: Wikidata, VIAF, ISNI, LCCN, IMDb, MusicBrainz, ISRC, ISWC, GeoNames, ROR, Getty AAT/TGN/ULAN.
